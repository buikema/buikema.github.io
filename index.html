Mill Pond Animal Hospital

About Us

Mill Pond Animal Hospital is proud to serve the Pinckney, Michigan area for everything pet related. Our veterinary clinic and animal hospital is run by Dr. John Diehr, who is a licensed, experienced veterinarian.

Our team is committed to educating our clients in how to keep your pets healthy year round, with good nutrition and exercise. Mill Pond Animal Hospital stays on top of the latest advances in veterinarian technology and above all, remembers that all animals and pets need to be treated with loving care in every check-up, procedure, or surgery.
